This Cookie Policy explains how NOAHSYSTEM Corporation ("NOAHSYSTEM," "we," "us," "our," or "the Company") uses cookies and similar technologies on its websites, online communities, game clients, and related marketplaces (collectively, the "Services"). By continuing to use our Services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Policy and our Privacy Policy.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow the Service to recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

We also use similar technologies, collectively referred to as "Cookies" in this Policy: Web Beacons (Pixel Tags), Local Storage, and Device Identifiers for tracking and analytics.

2. NOAHSYSTEM'S USE OF COOKIES

We use Cookies to ensure the technical performance and commercial viability of the Services, including:

  1. Security and Authentication: To verify log-in information, safeguard the security of our data and services, and detect cheating, hacking, and fraudulent activities.
  2. Administration & Performance: To administer the Services, track traffic flow, monitor system performance, and optimize the user experience.
  3. Personalization: To remember your preferences, language settings, and log-in details.
  4. Advertising: To deliver relevant NOAHSYSTEM promotions or third-party advertisements.

3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

We use both First-Party Cookies (set by NOAHSYSTEM directly) and Third-Party Cookies (set by partners). Cookies are classified by their necessity and legal basis:

Type Necessity and Legal Basis Purpose and Your Control
A. Strictly Necessary Necessary for the core functionality of the Service and to fulfill our contractual obligation towards you. Required for secure log-in, managing authentication, and preventing fraudulent activities. Consent is not required.
B. Performance/Analytics Based on your consent. Used for analyzing traffic and usage patterns. Helps us measure and improve Service performance. You can control these.
C. Functionality/Preference Based on your consent. Used for remembering user settings (e.g., language, region). Enables personalization and an enhanced user experience. You can control these.
D. Targeting/Advertising Based on your consent. Used for behavioral advertising. Tracks browsing habits to deliver tailored ads. You can control these.

4. INFORMATION COLLECTED THROUGH COOKIES

The cookies and similar technologies may collect the following specific types of information for the purposes mentioned above:

  • IP Address
  • Date and Time of connection to the Services
  • Information regarding the device used (e.g., operating system, browser type)
  • Language settings and preferences
  • Data about the pages visited and time spent on the Services
  • Links and elements you click on the Services

The use of non-essential cookies often results in the transfer of your data to servers located in Third Countries outside the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom, primarily the United States.

By providing your consent for non-essential cookies, you acknowledge and agree to this transfer. To protect your data, NOAHSYSTEM utilizes appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

7. MANAGING YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

You have control over non-essential cookies and can modify or withdraw your consent at any time.

7.1 Using the NOAHSYSTEM Cookie Preference Center

You can manage your consent through the Cookie Settings or Manage Preferences tool available on our Service interface (e.g., cookie banner, footer link). This allows you to selectively enable or disable cookies by category (Performance, Functionality, Targeting).

7.2 Managing Through Your Browser Settings

You can block or delete cookies by adjusting the settings in your web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari). However, disabling cookies, especially Strictly Necessary ones, may cause the Services to malfunction or prevent access to certain features.
